TomatoTimer

Welcome to my TomatoTimer!

TomatoTimer is an implementation of the Pomodoro techinque. The Pomodoro technique is a time management method, which focuses on medium-sized periods of intense work, which is then

followed by a short break, of about 5 minutes. These work periods are called Pomodoros. Every four Pomodoros, you take a long break, which is around 10 minutes.
